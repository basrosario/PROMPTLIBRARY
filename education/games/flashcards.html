How to Study

1. Read the Question

Look at the front of the card and try to recall the answer before flipping.

2. Flip to Check

Click the card or press Space to see the answer. Compare it to what you thought.

3. Navigate Cards

Use Previous/Next buttons or arrow keys to move through the deck.

4. Repeat & Test

Shuffle and study again, then take the quiz to test your knowledge!
