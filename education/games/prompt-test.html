    How Scoring Works

    Clarity (25 pts)

    Is your prompt clear and easy for AI to understand? Does it specify what you want?

    Audience (25 pts)

    Did you consider who the response is for? Did you specify the appropriate level or context?

    Completeness (25 pts)

    Does the AI's response cover the key topics? Did your prompt guide it to a complete answer?

    Quality (25 pts)

    Is the overall response useful and well-structured? Would it actually help with the task?

    Prompting Tips

    Tips for Better Prompts
    • Be Specific: Instead of "explain this topic," say "explain [topic] for a [audience] focusing on [aspects]"
    • Provide Context: Tell AI who you are, what you're working on, and why you need this
    • Set Format: Request specific formats like "in bullet points," "as a table," or "step by step"
    • Specify Length: Ask for "a brief summary" or "a detailed explanation" to control output length
    • Give Examples: Show AI what kind of response you want with an example
    • Iterate: If the first response isn't right, refine your prompt and try again
