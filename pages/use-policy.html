Acceptable Use Policy

How you can use, share, and build upon Praxis Library

Open to Everyone

Praxis Library is a free educational resource. No account, subscription, or payment is required. You can access all content, tools, and resources without restriction.

What You Can Do

Learn

Read, study, and practice with any content on the site. Use the interactive tools as many times as you like. All learning resources are yours to explore freely.

Teach

Use Praxis content in classrooms, workshops, training programs, and presentations. Reference framework explanations and glossary definitions in your own educational materials.

Share

Link to any page on the site. Share content on social media, in newsletters, or in professional communications. Recommend Praxis to colleagues, students, or communities.

Cite

Quote Praxis content with attribution. Use glossary definitions, framework descriptions, or methodology explanations in your own work with a link back to the source page.

Interactive Tools

All 7 interactive tools (Prompt Analyzer, Framework Finder, Preflight Checklist, Prompt Builder, Persona Architect, Hallucination Spotter, and Readiness Quiz) run entirely in your browser. Nothing you type is transmitted to any server. Your prompts, responses, and results stay on your device.

You can use these tools for personal learning, professional development, team training, or any purpose that helps you work with AI more effectively.

What We Ask You Not to Do

Misrepresent Authorship

Do not present Praxis content as your own original work. Attribution is simple: mention Praxis Library and link to the source page.

Remove Attribution

If you reproduce content from Praxis, keep the attribution intact. This helps others find the full resource and supports the open educational mission.

Use to Cause Harm

Do not use Praxis content to create misleading AI-generated content, to manipulate others, or to circumvent safety measures in AI systems. Praxis teaches responsible AI communication.
