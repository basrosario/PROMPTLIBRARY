Which Method Should I Use?

Method Best For Complexity Use When
CRISP Quick tasks, simple requests Low You need fast, focused results
CRISPE Creative work, persona-based tasks Medium You want consistent personality in responses
COSTAR Professional content, detailed outputs Medium Audience and tone matter significantly
ReAct Complex problems, multi-step reasoning High You need transparent reasoning process

Quick Tips for Better Prompts

01

Be Specific

Vague prompts get vague results. Include details about format, length, and style.

02

Provide Context

Tell the AI who you are, what you're working on, and why you need this output.

03

Give Examples

Show the AI what good output looks like. Examples are worth a thousand words.

04

Iterate & Refine

Great prompts are built through iteration. Start simple, then add complexity.
